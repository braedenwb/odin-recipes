White Cake

This simple cake recipe is te easiest, best-tasting cake you will ever make. It's great to make with kids. Use your favorite frosting on top of this delicious cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease and flour a 9-inch square cake pan.
  2. Cream sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl. Add eggs, one at a time, beating briefly after each addition. Mix in vanilla.
  3. Combine flour and baking powder in a seperate bowl. Add to the wet ingredients and mix well. Add milk and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs back when lightly touched, 30 to 40 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and cool completely. Frost and enjoy!
